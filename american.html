Located At: 92 S Main St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Billy Jacks

Enjoy some funky but just as delicious foods at one of the most popular restuarants in Harrisonburg, so popular the Jonas Brothers even stopped by for a beer. This restauant has unique burgers, scrumptious sandwiches, and more including tasty deserts, and even tastier beer for all to enjoy. You'll never find a restuarant like Billy Jacks anywhere else.


Jack Browns

Craving a burger? Jack Browns is your place. Enjoy some of the finest burgers and fries in this downtown area. Located right next to Billy Jacks, the two are a dynamic duo for any comfort American food! Sit either right by the bar and watch the chefs make your food right in front of you. Not your style? There's also the beautiful outside patio to enjoy your meal watching the downtown light up as the sun goes down.

Located At: 80 S Main Street, Harrisonburg, VA 22801



Urgies

Looking for a tasty of Philly? The best cheesesteaks in The Friendly City are located in a small restaurant just next to Urban Exchange Apartments here. Urgies loves their roots as the restuarant is decked out in Phillies "swag" alongside their amazing menu of all kinds of cheesesteaks. If you want a fun night to enjoy some cheesy goodness, make your way to Urgies, maybe you'll even catch a trivia night!

Located At: 245 E Water St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801
