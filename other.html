Located At: 350 University Blvd, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Islang Wing Company

Looking for a little bit of everything? Island Wing Company is a welcoming and fun place to enjoy all kinds of food with your friends or family. They pride themselves off not using the "F" word (fried) with their food. There menu varies from seafood to salads to burgers, there's something for everyone to enjoy when they stop by Island Wing. You won't miss going past it on the road by the brightly colored restaurant that makes you feel as if you're happy on an island cabana!

Sushi Village

Sushi Village is a unique experience for those looking for some good sushi in Virginia. Sushi Village is an all you can eat restuarant that makes you pay an entry fee and pay for leftover food once you're stuffed. They also include vegetarian options if that is how you enjoy eating. Reserve a table today and eat sushi!

Located At: 1820 Evelyn Byrd Ave, Ste 110, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Taste of India

This restaurant fills your nose with delicious smelling, traditional Indian food. This is also another all you can eat restaurant that offers vegan meal options if you prefer. These colorful meals are sure to get you excited for the buffet or combos they offer you at your wish.
