Italian Food


Bella Luna

Come enjoy some delicious Italian food over at Bella Luna! Specializing in pizza and pasta, this place is a gorgeous space to enjoy some food. You can watch the brick oven cook your pies while enjoying a nice cocktail from the bar in the center of the restaurant. Stop on over at Bella Luna but come quick, the place fills up fast!

Located At: 80 W Water St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Vito's Italian Kitchen

Vito's is a wonderful establishment to enjoy Italian cuisine. Food items including your staple Italian food including pasta, pizza, and sandwiches, enjoying it on their beautiful patio is a guaranteed great night once the weather is warm outside.

Little Italy's Pizza

This little joint down the road on South Main Street is one of Harrisonburgs hidden gems.
