Mexican Food


El Charro

Looking for a fun, cheap place to get some good Mexican food? El Charro is the place for you! With one of its two locations to choose from, it's a guaranteed fun time whenever you go. Try some of there 32 oz margaritas with a side of their chips and salsa and you won't want to leave!

Located at 1570 E Market St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801
& 1580 S Main St, Harrionsburg, VA 22801

Located at 14 E Water St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801

Magnolia's Taco & Tequilla Bar

Want a nicer mexican restaurant, Magnolias is your spot! This restuarant includes a rooftop view that is great for trying some of their delicious margaritas and tapas. Look over the downtown area on a warm night and it's the best way to unwind after a long day, or a short one.

Tequilla Taco Bar

If you and your friends want a place for a good margarita sampler and some fun, Tequilla is the place for you. This colorful building is always full of character and their food matches it. Try some of the appetizers or tequilla bowls, it's definitely a place where you get a show and a meal. Plus once a week they do a karoke night.

Located at 1007 S Main St, Harrisonburg, VA 22801
